SMS/Text Messaging Consent

OPT-IN CONSENT: By providing your mobile phone number and opting in to receive text messages, you authorize The Ancient Flour to send you text messages and notifications about our products, services, promotions, and other business-related communications. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe from text messages at any time.

Message Frequency: You may receive up to 4 messages per month, though message frequency may vary based on your interaction with our services and promotional campaigns.

No Third-Party Sharing: No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

The following elements relative to our 10DLC campaigns will not be shared or sold to third parties:
